Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Flour - 200 g
  • Milk - 250 ml
  • Egg - 1
  • Sugar - 2 tablespoons
  • Baking powder - 2 teaspoons
  • Butter - 30 g
  • Salt - 1 pinch

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the egg and milk together.
  3. Melt the butter and add it to the wet ingredients.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  5. Mix until a smooth batter forms.
  6. Heat a lightly greased skillet over medium heat.
  7. Pour a small amount of batter onto the skillet.
  8. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface.
  9. Flip the pancake and cook until golden brown.
  10. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  11. Serve warm with syrup, honey, or fresh fruit.
