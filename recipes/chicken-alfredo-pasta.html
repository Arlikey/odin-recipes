Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Creamy chicken Alfredo pasta with Parmesan cheese and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast - 2 pieces
  • Fettuccine pasta - 300 g
  • Heavy cream - 250 ml
  • Parmesan cheese - 100 g
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Butter - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste
  • Parsley - for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook the chicken until golden and fully cooked.
  5. Remove the chicken and slice it into strips.
  6. Finely chop the garlic.
  7. Add garlic to the skillet and cook for 1 minute.
  8. Pour in the cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  9. Add grated Parmesan cheese and stir until melted.
  10. Add the cooked pasta and mix well with the sauce.
  11. Return the chicken to the skillet and combine everything.
  12. Serve hot and garnish with chopped parsley.
